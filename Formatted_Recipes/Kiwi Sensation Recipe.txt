Title: Kiwi Sensation Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/250x250/950817.jpg
Rating: 4.0
Ingredients: 1.0 kiwi,0.5 cup pineapple chunks,0.5 cup green grapes,0.25 mango - peeled,0.25 cup water,0.5 cup spinach,2.0 cubes ice cubes
Duration: 10 m
Directions: Blend kiwi, pineapple, grapes, mango, and water in a blender until smooth; Add spinach and blend again until spinach is completely integrated; Add ice and blend until ice is completely integrated
