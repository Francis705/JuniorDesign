Title: Dragon Fruit Shake Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/1073093.jpg
Rating: 3.75
Ingredients: 1.0 dragon fruit (pitaya),2.0 tangerines,1.0 lime,4.0 leaves fresh basil,2.0 tablespoons brown sugar,1.0 cup sparkling mineral water,1.0 cup crushed ice
Duration: 10 m
Directions: Cut two 1/4 inch thick slices from the peeled dragon fruit to use as a garnish; set aside; Place the remaining dragon fruit into a blender along with the tangerine segments, lime juice, basil, brown sugar, and sparkling water; Puree until smooth; Stir in the crushed ice, and pour into glasses; Garnish with the reserved dragon fruit slices to serve
