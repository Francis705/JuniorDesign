Title: Vegan Smoothie Bowl with Carrot and Banana Recipe
Image: https://images.media-allrecipes.com/userphotos/560x315/3314783.jpg
Rating: 5.0
Ingredients: 2.0 pitted Medjool dates,1.0 frozen banana,1.0 cup coarsely chopped carrot,0.5 cup unsweetened vanilla-flavored almond milk,0.5 teaspoon ground cinnamon,0.25 teaspoon ground ginger,2.0 tablespoons flaked coconut,1.0 tablespoon goji berries
Duration: 15 m
Directions: Place dates in a small bowl and cover with cold water; let soak, about 5 minutes; Drain and chop
Directions: Place chopped dates, banana, carrot, almond milk, cinnamon, and ginger in a blender; puree until smoothie is thick and smooth; Pour into a serving bowl
Directions: Top smoothie bowl with flaked coconut and goji berries
