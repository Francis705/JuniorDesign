Title: Pie for Breakfast Shake Recipe

Ingredients: 0.75 cup frozen strawberries,0.5 cup vanilla coconut milk beverage,0.5 cup low-fat Greek-style yogurt,1.0 tablespoon flax seed meal,1.0 tablespoon almond butter,1.0 teaspoon honey,0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
Duration: 10 m
Directions: Blend strawberries, coconut milk, yogurt, flax seed, almond butter, honey, and vanilla in a blender until no traces of yogurt can be seen; Stop the blender and scrape down the sides with a rubber spatula; Continue blending until the mixture is completely smooth, about 1 more minute
